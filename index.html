The Process of Development

We approach programming in stages or iterations. Each
stage sets the pace for the subsequent iteration while
helping us to more carefully plan further work.
The storyboard is displayed on a corkboard to help our
manager to monitor development progress. This simple
process organization is a key to successful results.
We constantly assess our development pace by summing up
the number of completed tasks within each iteration. We
compose a list of tasks for the next iteration according to this
new data. Once we have the results of the iteration analysis,
we fine-tune and improve our performance for the next iteration.
